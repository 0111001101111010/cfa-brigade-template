We Are Code For Virginia Beach.

Come join developers, designers, leaders, and idea-makers who volunteer to help Virginia Beach Government and Civic Orgs adopt open technologies. We solve problems and want to increase citizen engagement with our City and each other.

Want To Help Your City? Good, we could use your help.

We are a Code for America Brigade, which means we're volunteers that contribute our talents toward improving the way our local governments and community organizations use the web. Brigade is about action and you're here because you are someone who takes action. See what kind of things brigades do, or watch the inspirational TED talk given by Jennifer Pahlka, the founder of Code for America.

We Meet Often. Come Join Us.

View our Meetups site for our next location and time. We try to meet every few Wednesdays where we sometimes hack (develop) our projects, do some project planning, or brainstorm on current issues or current Virginia Beach City activities. Thinking of coming? Great, we need many types of volunteers, and have a running list of ideas.
